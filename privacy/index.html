Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 April 2026

HomeBite ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

1. Who we are

HomeBite is a mobile application that helps you track your home food inventory, monitor expiry dates, and discover meals to cook. The app is operated by Gian Federspiel.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at: gian.federspiel@bluewin.ch

2. Information we collect

Account information. When you sign up, we collect your email address. If you use Sign in with Apple or Google Sign-In, we receive a unique identifier and, where you permit it, your name and email from those providers.

Food inventory data. Items you add to your pantry — including names, quantities, and expiry dates — are stored securely in our database.

Household data. If you create or join a household, we store the household name, its members, and shared inventory data.

Voice input. When you use voice to add items, audio is processed on-device using Apple's Speech Recognition framework. We do not receive or store your audio recordings.

Receipt photos. When you scan a receipt, the photo is sent to an optical character recognition (OCR) service to extract item names. The photo is processed in transit and is not stored by us or by the OCR service after processing.

Usage data. We may collect basic, anonymised information about how you use the app (e.g. features accessed) to improve the product. This data is not linked to your identity.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide and operate the HomeBite service
  • To generate personalised meal suggestions based on your pantry contents
  • To send expiry notifications you have opted in to receive
  • To enable household sharing between members you have invited
  • To improve the app based on aggregated, anonymised usage patterns

4. Third-party services

Supabase. We use Supabase to store your account and inventory data securely. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Supabase's privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

Anthropic (Claude AI). Your pantry contents are sent to Anthropic's API to generate meal suggestions. Anthropic processes this data in accordance with their usage policies. Pantry data sent to Anthropic is not used to train their models under our API agreement. Anthropic's privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy.

Apple Sign-In / Google Sign-In. If you use these sign-in methods, the respective provider's privacy policy applies to the authentication step.

Apple Speech Recognition. Voice input is processed using Apple's on-device Speech Recognition. Audio does not leave your device.

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

5. Data retention

We retain your account and inventory data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days. Anonymised, aggregated usage data may be retained indefinitely.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Export your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at gian.federspiel@bluewin.ch. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's privacy

HomeBite is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. If the changes are material, we will notify you within the app.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at gian.federspiel@bluewin.ch.
